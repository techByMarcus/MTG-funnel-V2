## Overview

Designed and launched a multi-step digital lead funnel focused on improving conversion rates, capturing qualified prospects, and reducing friction during the customer intake process.

This project was built to demonstrate real-world skills in:

- Lead generation systems
- User journey optimization
- Conversion-focused design
- Operations workflow thinking
- Analytics and testing
- Customer acquisition strategy

Rather than sending traffic directly to a generic landing page, this system guides users through a structured qualification flow designed to increase engagement and completion rates.

---

## Business Goal

Increase lead volume while lowering cost-per-lead through better user experience, stronger call-to-actions, and segmented intake logic.
